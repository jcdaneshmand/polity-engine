Polity Engine

Game system designed by Nigel Buckle and David Turczi.

Implemented by Jonah Daneshmand, Ph.D.

Polity Engine is an open-source, fan-made digital companion for Imperium-style table play. It helps with setup, solo practice, multiplayer lobbies, and local bookkeeping while encouraging players to support the official physical release.

Polity Engine does not include official card text, artwork, logos, scans, or component data. Players use physical content they own.
